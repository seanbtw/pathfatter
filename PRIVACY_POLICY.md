# Privacy Policy for PathFatter

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## Introduction

PathFatter ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PathFatter macOS application (the "App").

## Information Collection

### We Do NOT Collect

PathFatter operates with a **zero data collection** policy:

- ❌ We do **not** collect any personal information
- ❌ We do **not** collect usage data or analytics
- ❌ We do **not** track your paths or conversions
- ❌ We do **not** transmit any data to external servers
- ❌ We do **not** use third-party analytics or advertising services

### Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

1. **Path Mappings:** Your custom drive letter mappings (e.g., C: → /Volumes/C)
2. **SharePoint Mappings:** Your SharePoint URL prefixes and local OneDrive paths
3. **Conversion History:** Your recent path conversions (stored in UserDefaults)
4. **Pinned Items:** Paths you've chosen to pin for quick access

All data is stored exclusively on your Mac and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Data Storage

- **Storage Location:** macOS UserDefaults (sandboxed application container)
- **Access:** Only PathFatter can access this data
- **Retention:** Data persists until you delete the App or manually clear it
- **Backup:** May be included in your macOS backups (iCloud, Time Machine)

## Third-Party Services

PathFatter does **not** integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The App:

- Does not use analytics SDKs (Firebase, Fabric, etc.)
- Does not use advertising SDKs
- Does not use crash reporting services
- Does not make network requests for tracking purposes

## Safari Web Extension

If you use the optional Safari Web Extension:

- The extension operates within Safari's sandbox
- It only accesses the active tab URL when you trigger path conversion
- No browsing data is collected or transmitted
- The extension cannot access other tabs or browsing history

## Children's Privacy

PathFatter is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App and on our GitHub repository with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. All your data is stored locally on your device and is under your control.
