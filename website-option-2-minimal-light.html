How It Works

Three simple steps to path conversion bliss

1

Paste Your Path

Copy any Windows path (e.g., C:\Users\name\file.txt) or SharePoint URL and paste it into PathFatter.

2

Instant Conversion

PathFatter automatically converts it to the macOS equivalent (e.g., /Volumes/C/Users/name/file.txt).

3

Copy or Open

Click to copy the result or open the location directly in Finder. Done!
